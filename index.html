Akhilesh Kumar

Biomechanics Researcher

I am a biomechanics researcher broadly interested in understanding how biological structures generate, transmit, and regulate mechanical forces across scales — from cells and tissues to whole-body movement. My work integrates experimental and computational approaches to uncover the physical principles underlying human and animal locomotion, injury, and performance. I aim to translate these findings into practical applications in rehabilitation, sports science, and biomedical engineering.
